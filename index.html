Supported browsers:

  • Microsoft Edge
  • Firefox 28 and newer
  • Google Chrome
  • Safari 9 and newer (untested)
  • Opera 15 and newer
  • IOS 10 & 11
  • Android 5 and newer (untested)

There is a known bug with iOS 12 and some versions of the Safari with downloading

Older browsers including Internet Explorer 10 and earlier are not supported.

Internet Explorer 11 is not supported due to Discord authentication screen not supported, however any other link will work in IE11 once authenticated to Discord
